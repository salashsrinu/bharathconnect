Agriculture, Rural & Environment

  • Home
  • Categories
Agriculture remains the backbone of India's economy, especially in rural regions where it significantly contributes to GDP and provides livelihoods for a vast population. This vital sector embraces sustainable practices like soil conservation and actively promotes biodiversity through landmark initiatives such as the Green, White, Yellow, and Blue Revolutions.

The agricultural sector encompasses a wide spectrum, including crop production, resource management (water, land), agricultural research and development, efficient market linkages, animal husbandry, fisheries, and sustainable environmental management. It plays a crucial role in ensuring food security for the nation and driving economic growth.
