Website of Transport Department of Puducherry

Find information on citizen charter for services related to driving license like learners license, additional endorsement; motor vehicle registration services like transfer of ownership, renewal of R.C. and for permit services such as goods carriage, renewal of permit, etc. Users can go through documents such as Motor Vehicle Act, Motor Vehicle Rules, new notifications, VAT circular, fancy registration number, etc. Information is available on road safety, tax structure and penalty for tax, traffic signs, forms, fee, crisis management, bus, auto-rickshaw fares, etc. Links for routes of ladies special buses; smart card and High Security Registration Plates (HSRP); NOC issue details; new vehicle registration numbers and road tax are also provided. Details of departments, regional offices, contact numbers, organisation chart, etc. are also available.

Information on traffic rules by Transport Department of Rajasthan

Information on traffic rules and regulations is provided by Transport Department of Rajasthan. Get details about road signs. Details on mandatory road signs such as no entry, one way, restrictions and prohibitions, compulsory instructions are provided. Information on warning signs for safety such as intersections, crossings, bends, etc. is available. Users can go through informatory signs like parking signs. Information is also given about line markings on the road such as separation of lanes etc.

Urban Transport Programme by Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs

Find programmes related to Urban Transport by Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs. Details are available for National Urban Transport Policy, metro rails and mass transit systems, traffic and transportation policies, funding of buses under JnNURM, capacity building in urban transport, awards for excellence, National Common Mobility Card (NCMC) etc. Acts related to urban transport such as Metro Railways (Construction of Works) Act, 1978 are also provided. Notifications, studies, reports on urban transport are also given.

Website of Transport Department of Haryana

Transport Department of Haryana provides efficient, reliable and eco-friendly modern transport services. User can get details related to vehicle registration, driving licence, permits, driving schools, driver training institute, etc. Information about road safety, seat belt, pollution control, use of helmet, pollution check centres, concessional travelling, etc. is given. Access to details on roadways, bus stand enquiry numbers, advance booking counters, cloak rooms, Yatri Niwas, bus time table, etc. Users can book their tickets online through e-ticket system.

Website of Ministry of Road Transport and Highways

Get information on Ministry of Road Transport and Highways. Users can get details related to Road Transport, National Highways and Transport Research in the country. Information on road safety, National Highways, road transport, national permit, NHDP maps, immovable property returns, etc. is given. Details on the Ministry, its associated offices, rules, notifications, Right to Information, vacancies, etc. are also available.
