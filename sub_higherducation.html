Form for North Eastern Council Scholarship (new application) in Meghalaya

Get application form for merit scholarship, stipend and book-grant for the students of Meghalaya under the North Eastern Council Scholarship scheme. Students have to fill the personal and other information such as name, address, phone number, details of the school, caste, etc. Details of documents to be attached with this form are also given.

Forms by Delhi University

Users can find downloadable forms to avail various services provided by the Delhi University. Forms are provided for degree/transcript, change name, DUCC forms, WUS health centre forms, employees and insurance scheme etc.

Website of State Board of Technical Education, Jharkhand

The State Board of Technical Education is responsible for formulating new courses and Developing Curricula besides working for advancement of Technical Education and Research work. The board is also responsible to conduct examination, provide certification and advise the State Government regarding the development of Technical Education and Art & crafts.

Application for Issuance of Duplicate Certificates Class VIII, IX, X, XII in Nagaland

This form is to be used by students to apply for duplicate certificate can apply for Class X and XII certificates. NBSE requires a student to furnish various details such as newspaper advertisement, scanned copy of FIR Certificate, Proof of identity etc which will be validated by the Department to issue a Duplicate Certificate. The Duplicate Certificate is issued by the Nagaland Board of School Education for class VIII, IX, X & XII.

Scholarships by Directorate of Higher Education of Assam

List of scholarships given by the Directorate of Higher Education, Assam to undergraduate and post graduate students are provided. Details about different scholarships, such as M.Phil scholarship, Ph.D. scholarship, Combine Merit and National Merit scholarships, etc are available. Details on Higher Education in Assam, Mandate of Higher Education, Progress during Five Year Plans, Administrative Structure, Profile of Posts in Colleges, Pension cases forwarded to Accountant General is also made available here.
