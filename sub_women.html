Early Childhood Education for 3-6 Age Group Children

Find details of the Early Childhood Education for 3-6 Age Group Children under the Programme of Universalisation of Elementary Education. The scheme is funded by the Central Government and provided by the Ministry of Women and Child Development. Scheme related details such as eligibility, beneficiary, required documents, application procedure and forms are available. Contact details of the coordinating agency are also provided.

Website of Assam State Commission For Protection of Child Rights

The Assam State Commission for Protection of Child Rights has been constituted for protection, promotion, development, survival and participation of children. Users can find information about the functions of the Commission, Right to Education Cell, activities to protect children etc. Detailed information on how to file a complaint relating to violation of children rights is also provided. Information about the vacancies and internships is also given.

Website of Mahila Samakhya Society of Chhattisgarh

Mahila Samakhya programme launched by Ministry of Education aims to educate and empower women in rural areas, particularly of women from socially and economically marginalised groups. Information about programme, society, its vision, mission and structure, etc. is given. Access to case study is also available.

Women Helpline Numbers of Delhi Police

Get women helpline numbers of Delhi Police. Users can access women helpline numbers for various districts of Delhi such as New Delhi District, North District, North-West District, South District, West District, etc. Information about name of the police stations and their women helpline number is available.

Contact details of State Commission for Women

Find the contact details of the state and Union Territories Commission for Women by the National Commission for Women (NCW). Information about the chairperson, members along with the name, office and residence address, email, website and other contact details is available.
