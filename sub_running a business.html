Skill Development in 47 Districts Affected By Left Wing Extremism Program

The aim is to create skill development infrastructure in the districts closer to the people of left wing extremism (LWE) affected districts. The objective is to establish one ITI & two Skill Development Centers (SDCs) in each of the districts and to run demand driven vocational training courses, both long and short term, to meet the requirement of skilled manpower of various sectors.

Website of National Single Window System (NSWS) for Business Approvals
